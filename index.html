The main departments in the hospital

Al-Hakim Hospital's emergency room doctor and nurse welcome you as soon as you walk in the door, your urgency is determined immediately and medical intervention begins without wasting time. After that, diagnosis, follow-up and treatment are carried out in a multidisciplinary manner with all types of laboratory support and advanced imaging in the process carried out by the relevant specialist physician.

The oral and dental health staff at Al-Hakim Hospital consists of expert and competent dentists in each branch. In the clinic, current technological and aesthetic applications are implemented in a scientific and patient-oriented manner. A team of expert dentists from every branch of dentistry is always at your service for your oral health.

General Surgery Clinic at Al-Hakim Hospital; It brings together doctors specialized in branches such as endocrine surgery, breast pathology and surgery, liver and biliary surgery, peripheral vascular surgery, varicose veins, and obesity surgery. These branches offer teamwork to their patients, together with the Departments of Oncology, Radiology, Plastic Surgery and Psychiatry, with a multidisciplinary approach within them.

Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology, Al-Hakim Hospital; It aims to achieve the highest success rate in treating all diseases related to women’s health, the changes they suffer from before and after pregnancy, during menopause, and gynecological cancers, which represent every woman’s nightmare.

Initial Clinical Examination

Mon, November 20th 2023
The initial clinical examination is extremely important in diagnosing health conditions and determining treatment needs. This examination allows doctors to examine physical symptoms and signs, which helps detect early diseases and determine subsequent care plans. The clinical examination includes examination of blood pressure, heart, ears, and eyes, and contributes to identifying general health problems. This measure is considered essential for prevention and comprehensive health care, and contributes to enhancing health awareness and ensuring an effective response to any health challenges that may arise.

Maintaining The Nervous System

Mon, November 23th 2023
The nervous system plays a vital role in the body's functions, so periodic nerve examination is necessary for early detection of its diseases. This includes a comprehensive examination of nerve function and sensory and motor response tests. Regular examination can diagnose problems such as neuropathy, chronic nerve disease, and peripheral nervous system disease. This approach contributes to detecting early symptoms and developing appropriate treatment plans. Through regular screening, nerve problems can be identified before they cause larger health problems, promoting comprehensive care and improving quality of life.

Regular Eye Examination

Mon, November 20th 2023
Regular eye examination is of great importance in maintaining healthy vision and preventing eye diseases. This examination helps detect vision problems early, allowing appropriate treatment and limiting the development of diseases. Regular examination also enables the diagnosis of general health problems that may affect the eyes, such as diabetes and high blood pressure. In addition, regular examination provides an opportunity to correct refractive errors by prescribing glasses or contact lenses, which contributes to improving vision and comfort of the eyes. Conducting this examination regularly enhances awareness of the importance of eye health care and contributes to maintaining visual quality of life.

Newborn Health

Mon, November 20th 2023
The health of newborns is essential to their development and growth. Health care for infants includes periodic examinations to check their normal growth and development, and to evaluate the functions of vital organs and systems. Good nutrition and necessary vaccinations contribute to strengthening immunity and preventing diseases. Early detection of any health problems plays an important role in providing appropriate treatment and avoiding possible complications. Encouraging good mental and physical health care, in addition to providing a safe environment, contributes to ensuring a strong healthy start for newborns.
